Enter the motion properties and the values of an object to see the Graphical representation of the object during the motion. Here is a Magic... You have to give any 3 data from Primary Velocity(u), Final Velocity(v) , Time(t) and Acceleration(a). This automatically calculate the 4rth Property and show you the Graphical Representation. You also can access these in any supported Units like SI and CGI (Till now available).
